Workout Plan for Underweight Persons

Customized workout plan for males and females

For Males

  • Exercise 1: Push-ups

    Sets: 4

    Reps: 15

  • Exercise 2: Bodyweight Squats

    Sets: 4

    Reps: 12

  • Exercise 3: Dumbbell Rows

    Sets: 3

    Reps: 12

  • Exercise 4: Barbell Bench Press

    Sets: 3

    Reps: 10

For Females

  • Exercise 1: Planks

    Sets: 3

    Duration: 30 seconds

  • Exercise 2: Leg Raises

    Sets: 3

    Reps: 12

  • Exercise 3: Lateral Leg Raises

    Sets: 3

    Reps: 12 (each leg)
